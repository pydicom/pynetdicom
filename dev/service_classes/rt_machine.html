RT Machine Verification Service Class¶

The RT Machine Verification Service Class defines a service that uses the DIMSE N-CREATE, N-SET, N-DELETE, N-ACTION, N-EVENT-REPORT and N-GET services to facilitate the independent verification of geometric and dosimetric settings on a radiation delivery system prior to delivery of treatment.
