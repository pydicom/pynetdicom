Unified Procedure Step Service Class#

The Unified Procedure Step Service Class defines a service that uses the DIMSE C-FIND, N-CREATE, N-SET, N-ACTION, N-EVENT-REPORT and N-GET services to facilitate management of simple worklists, including creation of new worklist items, querying worklists and communicating progress and results.
